zoomoid is a young music artist who has grown up with music always by his side. As commercial electronic music began to rise up, he developed interest and decided to attempt producing stuff on his own.

He eventually got very attracted to house music and its subgenres and produces his very own music ever since.

Besides, he listens to a hell lot of other music, making it nearly impossible for him to choose a favorite song. If he really had to choose, his answer would be 'Goodbye To A World' by Porter Robinson, because he thinks it's the greatest and most beautiful song of all time. Also, he recommends listening to Bach.
