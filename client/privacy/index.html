We value your privacy the most. Since this is a static website, there are no cookies transmitted between our servers and you, you cannot send any personal data to us, there are no forms included in this website and our server do not permanently log any access or error data (webdevs: is this bad?).

This however may not hold true for embedded content. YouTube, Spotify, etc. may store cookies from your sessions or require you to submit some sort of data. Note however, that this traffic does not get routed through our servers and thus, the linked platforms are responsible for providing you with the information they store on you and deleting it, if so requested by you.

If concerns regarding your privacy still arise and you would like to ensure no data is stored, simply ask us and we will grant you access to the source code of the site.
